Bio

I am a last-year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Universite Laval (UL) researching under supervision of Christian Gagne (the director of Insitute Intelligence and Data and a MILA's professor) and Denis Laurendeau (the director of Computer Vision and System Lab ). My current research revolves around dependeble and secure learning models to adversarial examples and Out-of-Distribution samples. Before joining UL, I was a researcher master student at Digitial Media Lab of Sharif University of Technology(SUT), working on 3D human pose estimation under supervision H. Reza. Rabiee. I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from SUT. As my BSc project, I studied and investigated the community detection algorithms for detecting communities of Iranian Mathematicians around the globe.
